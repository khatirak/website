Projects

Film

We decided to create a 'How to' video tutorial focused on making the perfect matcha latte. This project involved planning the video structure, filming the step-by-step process, and editing to create an engaging and informative tutorial that demonstrates the art of matcha preparation.

Sound

We decided to recreate tense and exciting moments in a basketball match, incorporating wins, losses, and interactive elements into the website. This project involved sound design, creating an immersive audio experience that captures the energy and intensity of a live basketball game, with interactive features that allow users to experience different game scenarios and outcomes.

Comic

We created an interactive story featuring a walrus character inspired by Where's Wally. This project combines visual storytelling with user choice, allowing readers to navigate through different story paths and choose the direction of Wally's adventure. The comic features engaging illustrations and multiple narrative branches that create a unique reading experience for each user.

30MFF

This is a yearly tradition at NYU Abu Dhabi where students create a 90-second film about something on campus. I decided to focus my film on Lingua Coffee, the campus cafe. The project involved capturing the essence of this social hub, documenting the daily life, conversations, and community that forms around this central gathering place, telling a story of connection and campus culture in just 90 seconds.
